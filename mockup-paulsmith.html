Eiffel Tower
Wikipedia · simple.wikipedia.org
An iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. Built 1887–1889 by Gustave Eiffel as the entrance arch for the 1889 World's Fair. At 330m it was the world's tallest structure for 41 years.
